Project

Thank you for visiting our website, we are young and ambitious team, trying to make a difference. We specialize in lifting devices for indoor use. On this website you will have an opportunity to observe and purchase our new prototype.

We believe that our product design can incorporate, three main aspects: Quality, Efficiency and Safety. This result was achieved through continuous research and work. You can find out more about it here.

Quality

Each separate part was chosen carefully by our engineers. We always seek to find the middle ground between quality and price, however if we had to choose between these two options, quality would always be a priority. Considering our field research, we pursued a goal of having a construction that could be able to endure continues stress without decreasing its longevity.

Efficiency

Our product incorporates multiple functional design. The product at hand, can be used both for lifting and rehabilitation purposes. We would like to provide a product that gives an opportunity for disabled people to walk again, without spending extra money, for additional equipment. Furthermore, being of aluminum construction, product is quite mobile. It is light and easy to move around. We made sure, that it would easily go through doors in medical institutions.

Safety

Of course, dealing with immobile people it is important to ensure that all safety measures would be taken into account. Your safety, is of our highest priority. To guarantee it, we have meticulously researched that our product would be stable and firm on the ground. The product can lift up to 300 kg without losing any balance.
